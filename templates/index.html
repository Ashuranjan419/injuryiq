About the System

InjuryIQ is an advanced AI-powered platform designed specifically for football coaches and medical staff. Our system leverages cutting-edge machine learning algorithms to predict injury recovery times and assess setback risks for injured players.

By analyzing factors such as injury type, severity, player age, position, fitness level, and injury history, our models provide accurate predictions that help teams:

  • ⚽ Plan training schedules effectively
  • 🎯 Make informed decisions about player availability
  • 💪 Optimize rehabilitation programs
  • 🛡️ Reduce the risk of re-injury
  • 🏆 Improve overall team performance
